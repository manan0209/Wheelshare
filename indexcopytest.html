About us



The "Wheel Share" project streamlines the process of ride-sharing, fostering a connected community and promoting eco-friendly commuting practices.
The platform will prioritize user experience and implement robust security measures, providing a reliable and secure environment for users to share rides and reduce their carbon footprint.
Moving forward, the "Wheel Share" team is committed to expanding the platform and enhancing its features, aiming to create a more sustainable and interconnected future for transportation.
